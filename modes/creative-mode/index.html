Creative Mode

Current answer

Creative Mode is a live builder-focused way to play Dragonwilds. It was introduced with 0.9 and is designed for construction, experimentation and custom world control.

What it changes

Creative Mode reduces the friction around building and pairs with configurable world settings. It is suitable for testing layouts and creating structures without treating every survival rule as fixed.

What it is not

Creative Mode is separate from a public demo and from combat activities such as Fight Cave. World owners should decide whether a creative ruleset matches the experience they want before starting a shared world.
