Mining and Woodcutting Storage Unlock at Level 59

Published from official information dated 2026-06-22; editorial status reviewed 2026-07-19.

What changed

Both gathering tracks gain a specialized storage building at level 59, reducing pressure on mixed general storage.

Confirmed details

  • Ore and Stone Storage is unlocked through Mining level 59.
  • Lumber Storage is unlocked through Woodcutting level 59.
  • The buildings are part of the expanded level 50–99 skill progression.

What this means for players

These unlocks reward targeted skilling rather than random exploration. Groups can assign a gatherer to reach the recipe while coordinating where the new storage sits relative to processing and crafting stations.

Player checklist

  1. Check the relevant skill level and recipe unlock.
  2. Plan a storage zone near the matching processing route.
  3. Move materials only after the building is placed and verified.
