Infinity Cloth and Four Vestige Sets Added

Published from official information dated 2026-06-22; editorial status reviewed 2026-07-19.

What changed

Infinity Cloth and Vestige gear broaden endgame equipment choices beyond the basic Adamant and Dragonhide material paths.

Confirmed details

  • Infinity Cloth can be spun into Infinity Robes.
  • Magic receives Lunar Garou and Desert Robes Vestige sets.
  • Ranged receives Kalphite Carapace and melee receives Obsidian.

What this means for players

Vestige equipment belongs to build-specific progression, so “best armour” comparisons need to state combat role and activity. A generic tier list that ignores magic, ranged and melee identities will mislead players.

Player checklist

  1. Choose the intended combat style first.
  2. Confirm the Mystic Forge or Armour Bench recipe location.
  3. Save rare components until the complete set requirement is verified.

Official source and status

This article summarizes confirmed information and does not add unannounced mechanics, drop rates or release promises. Future-facing features remain labelled as announced until they are live.
