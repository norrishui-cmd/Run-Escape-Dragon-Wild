Traders Added Across All of Ashenfall

Published from official information dated 2026-06-22; editorial status reviewed 2026-07-19.

What changed

The trader expansion gives surplus materials and loot a new use instead of leaving every excess item in storage.

Confirmed details

  • Traders are added across all of Ashenfall.
  • They accept some materials and spare loot.
  • Available exchanges depend on the individual shop rather than one universal list.

What this means for players

Players should compare the actual live shop before building a farming route. Patch notes confirm the system broadly but do not establish permanent prices for every merchant or item.

Player checklist

  1. Visit traders encountered on normal regional routes.
  2. Compare sale value with future crafting needs.
  3. Record patch-specific prices rather than assuming all shops match.

Official source and status

This article summarizes confirmed information and does not add unannounced mechanics, drop rates or release promises. Future-facing features remain labelled as announced until they are live.
