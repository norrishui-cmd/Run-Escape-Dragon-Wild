Nivalis Nights Business Partnerships Guide

How business partnerships fit the relationship system and can create economic opportunities.

Quick answer: Business partnership is one of four confirmed relationship paths. These relationships can change the economic experience and open opportunities, although named partners and benefits are not public before release.

A distinct relationship type

The Q&A lists business partnerships alongside friendship, rivalry and romance. This means economic cooperation is not merely a generic friendship label, but not every character is confirmed to support it.

Possible effects, careful limits

ION LANDS confirms economic impact and new opportunities, with vendor pricing as one example. It does not promise shared ownership, passive income, exclusive stock or loans, so those possibilities remain speculation.

Guide evidence standard

Partner pages will document prerequisites, dialogue or quest choices, benefits, conflicts and reversibility. Economic claims must be reproduced or directly sourced, especially when market events could change prices.
