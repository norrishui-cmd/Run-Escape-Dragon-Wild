Confirmed story character

Icthlarin

Current answer

Icthlarin is linked to the alliance with the Moon Garou in the live Umbral Sands storyline. Official 0.12 material confirms that role without publishing a full character biography.

Story context

The Moon Garou struggle to recover after Fuzan destroyed their civilization. Icthlarin’s involvement connects the player to this desert faction while the broader conflict with Fuzan, Kalphites and Kot’Haar unfolds.

Evidence boundary

This entry records only the role stated in the official update. Dialogue choices, exact quest steps and rewards should be taken from the current game rather than inferred from wider RuneScape lore.
