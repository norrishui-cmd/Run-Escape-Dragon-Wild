Nivalis Nights Characters and Relationships

Confirmed information about NPCs, friendships, rivals, romance and the future character guide database.

Quick answer: Nivalis Nights features a large cast with persistent daily schedules. Players can form friendships, make enemies, manage relationships and potentially find love.

A city with persistent inhabitants

The developer says thousands of NPCs continue living their lives across districts. A three-tier “Ghost system” tracks out-of-district schedules, nearby unseen characters and fully visible NPCs differently to preserve performance. Not every simulated citizen will justify a search page; named characters with quests, relationships or services will.

Character page standard

A character guide needs an exact name, role, locations or schedule, relationship mechanics, connected quests and verified choices. Romance status, gifts and event triggers must come from official information or play testing.
