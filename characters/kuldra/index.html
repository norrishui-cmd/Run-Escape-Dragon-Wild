Kuldra, the God-Eater

Current answer

Kuldra is an announced 1.0 raid boss, not a live Early Access encounter as of this review. Jagex describes the battle as the culmination of Kuldra’s Saga.

Confirmed role

The raid advances through floating islands and wild anima before confronting Kuldra. The official roadmap associates the encounter with Dragon equipment rewards, but has not published final drop rates or a complete encounter script.

Announced rewards

  • Latest Dragon Shield and a cape.
  • Pieces of Dragon armour.
  • Dragon Scimitar and Dragon Crossbow.
  • Additional secret rare drops that remain undisclosed.
