## Competitor and SERP signals

- `nivalisnights.org` has built a visually rich launch page and targets districts, daily schedules and FAQ queries, but it also publishes exact day-one times, credits and quest/location claims that need stronger verification.
- `nivalis-nights.wiki` and The Games Wiki are also appearing for broad wiki queries.
- The opportunity is to win on evidence-backed long tails, clearer system pages and better hub-to-leaf navigation rather than copying unverified trailer interpretations.

## Monetization readiness

- The 90-page structure now supports future ad placement without forcing ads into empty database templates.
- Highest commercial-intent pre-release pages are system requirements, GPU/RAM/storage, Steam versus Epic, platform status, price/editions and release date.
- Ad density should remain low until indexed sessions and engagement data justify layout testing.
