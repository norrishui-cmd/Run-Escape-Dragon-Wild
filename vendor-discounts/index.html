How Vendor Discounts Work in Nivalis Nights

What is confirmed about earning better vendor prices through repeated purchases and business relationships.

Quick answer: Buying regularly from certain vendors may lead to better prices. ION LANDS presents this as an example of how business relationships can affect the player economically, not as a universal guaranteed discount.

The official wording is conditional

The developer says buying more often from certain vendors “might” get better prices. That leaves room for vendor-specific rules, relationship thresholds or story choices rather than one global loyalty meter.

Discounts versus cheap items

A lower observed price does not prove a relationship discount because market events can also change behavior. Release testing should compare the same item, vendor and market conditions over time.

Verification method

A reliable guide will record purchase count, dialogue changes, relationship state, base price and discounted price. It will also test whether discounts expire, stack or apply to every product category.
