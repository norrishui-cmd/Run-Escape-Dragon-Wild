How Apartment Rental Works in Nivalis Nights

Confirmed rental limits, locations and furnishing rules for apartments across Nivalis.

Quick answer: Players can rent apartments in different parts of the city and may rent as many as their wallet permits. A strategically located home can make travel around Nivalis more convenient.

No announced fixed property limit

The August 2025 housing preview ties the number of rentals to available money rather than naming a hard cap. This does not reveal deposits, recurring rent, eviction rules or whether every residence uses the same contract.

Location has practical value

The developer explicitly says a home in a strategic location can improve movement around the city. That matters in a district-based map where travel can involve HOVA taxis, trains and loading screens.

What tenants can change

Some rentals include basic furniture, and players can place furniture and decorations to personalize apartments. Exact storage limits, fast-travel behavior and whether furnishings persist after ending a rental require final-game testing.
