Can You Own Multiple Properties in Nivalis Nights?

How multiple restaurants, venues, apartments and standalone houses work across Nivalis districts.

Quick answer: Yes. Players can purchase multiple restaurants or venues, plus apartments and standalone houses in different districts. Each district’s properties have their own style.

Business expansion

Owning more than one restaurant or venue is part of expanding the business into new districts. Staff, ingredients and upgrades make that expansion a management problem rather than a cosmetic collection alone.

Residential ownership

The community Q&A separately confirms apartments and standalone houses across the city. Existing official features also allow players to decorate their homes and customize spaces for friends or paying clients.

Progression limits

Exact property counts, prices and prerequisites are not yet published. Individual property URLs will wait for verified names, locations and benefits.
