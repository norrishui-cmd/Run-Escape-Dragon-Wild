Survival

Survival Systems

This hub connects the practical systems players repeat every session: gather, craft, cook, repair, build, scout, return, and upgrade.

Core Loops to Document

  • Food and healing preparation before longer routes.
  • Tool and gear repair checks before dungeons or dragon content.
  • Storage and station placement that reduces wasted base time.
  • Exploration loops that return through known safe areas.

What Not to Fake

Exact food values, enemy damage, station recipes, and drop rates should only be added when verified in-game or from official notes.

Desert survival changes the loop

Umbral Sands adds Sunscorch and quicksand. Prolonged sun exposure applies Scorch, which rapidly drains hydration; shelter, shade and travel at dawn or dusk are the confirmed counters. Quicksand makes direct map routes unsafe.

Late-game survival therefore still depends on route planning, not just armour. Use the Sunscorch checklist and quicksand route guide before building resource loops across the desert.
