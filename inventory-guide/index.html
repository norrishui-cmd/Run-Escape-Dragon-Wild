Nivalis Nights Inventory Guide

The confirmed personal, kitchen, greenhouse and home inventories in Nivalis Nights.

Quick answer: Nivalis Nights uses multiple inventories: the player’s personal inventory, restaurant kitchens, rented greenhouses and homes each have their own storage context.

Separate storage contexts

The developer’s community Q&A explicitly identifies personal, kitchen, greenhouse and home inventories. This means resource planning involves where an item is stored, not only the total quantity owned.

Why transfers matter

Ingredients can move from buying or greenhouse production into restaurant use, while harvested goods can also be sold. Exact transfer rules, remote access and stack limits are not yet public and should not be assumed.

Launch guide fields

The completed guide will document capacity, sorting, filters, item movement, storage upgrades and whether staff can access kitchen stock automatically.
