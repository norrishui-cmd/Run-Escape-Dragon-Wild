Can an RTX 2070 Run Nivalis Nights?

How the RTX 2070 appears in the official recommended Nivalis Nights specification.

Quick answer: Yes. Steam gives “RTX 2070 or newer” as an example for the recommended graphics tier, which also specifies 8 GB dedicated VRAM and DirectX 12.

What recommended means

The listing does not attach a resolution, frame rate or quality preset to the RTX 2070 example. It confirms the card class but not a 60 FPS guarantee at a particular setting.

Other required components

The recommended setup also lists 16 GB RAM, Windows 10 or 11 64-bit, 30 GB storage and an SSD. The CPU requirement remains generic rather than naming a model.

Benchmark policy

Release tests should report resolution, preset, district, weather and crowd conditions. An average frame rate without those details would be misleading.
