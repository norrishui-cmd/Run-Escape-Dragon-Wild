Nivalis Nights Greenhouse Automation

How temperature, humidity, light and automated irrigation affect greenhouse management.

Quick answer: Greenhouse consoles let players configure temperature, humidity and light, which directly influence crop growth speed. Irrigation is one of the processes that can be automated.

Environmental controls

The farming console is designed for optimizing cultivation and yield. Temperature, humidity and lighting are adjustable rather than fixed background effects, giving greenhouse management its own systems layer.

Machinery and modules

Players can install specialized machinery bought from city vendors and unlock advanced modules as farming develops. The developer has confirmed automation but has not published exact thresholds or optimal settings.

Testing plan

Release guides should compare one variable at a time, record crop, growth duration and yield, and distinguish convenience upgrades from profit increases. Generic “best settings” claims will wait for reproducible data.
