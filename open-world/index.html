Is Nivalis Nights Open World?

How the large district structure, transportation, interiors and story unlocks shape Nivalis Nights exploration.

Quick answer: Nivalis Nights is tagged Open World on Steam, but the developer describes a district-based city: large areas are freely explorable, with loading screens and automated travel between districts.

Free exploration within districts

Players can enter most business interiors and move around each district freely. Boats open access to water-connected areas, while story progression unlocks additional locations.

Not one seamless map

HOVA taxis and trains handle district changes, and the developer explicitly confirms loading screens between districts. “Open world” therefore should not be interpreted as a single seamless space.

Story structure

The game also has a defined main story and specific ending. Open-ended business and life-sim activities exist alongside that directed narrative.
