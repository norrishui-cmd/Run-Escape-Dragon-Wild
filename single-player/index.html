Is Nivalis Nights Single Player?

Nivalis Nights is listed as a single-player game on Steam; here is what that confirms and what it does not.

Quick answer: Yes. Steam marks Nivalis Nights as Single-player. The public listing does not currently show an online co-op or multiplayer feature badge.

How the city stays alive

Single-player does not mean the city is static. ION LANDS describes thousands of NPCs with daily schedules, persistent objects, staff activity across districts and relationships that respond to choices.

Avoiding unsupported claims

This page reports current storefront features. It does not claim that multiplayer can never be added; it only states that no such mode is currently listed for launch.
