Nivalis Nights Recipe Customization

How recipe unlocks and customization connect ingredients to restaurant management.

Quick answer: Players unlock new recipes as they progress and can customize them, adding another decision layer to restaurant operations.

Beyond fixed recipes

The developer distinguishes unlocking recipes from customizing them. That suggests meals are not only static collection entries, although the exact ingredient substitution or quality rules are not yet public.

Supply connection

Ingredients can be purchased or produced in greenhouses, then stored in kitchens and used by restaurant businesses. Custom recipes should therefore be evaluated for supply cost as well as sale value.

Future recipe tests

Each recipe page will need base ingredients, customization options, unlock source, preparation requirements and effect on customers or profit.
