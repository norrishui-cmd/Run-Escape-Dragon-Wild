Nivalis Nights Platforms

Current Nivalis Nights platform status for Steam, Epic Games Store, consoles and Steam Deck.

Quick answer: The confirmed dated release is Windows PC via Steam on September 29, 2026. The official trailer says Epic Games Store will follow later. Console versions are not confirmed on the current official Steam listing.

PC storefronts

Steam has the active product page, release date, requirements, languages and feature badges. Epic Games Store is announced for a later date, but no dated Epic page is cited in the current official release material.

Console and handheld status

Do not treat PlayStation, Xbox or Nintendo pages from third parties as confirmation unless ION LANDS or 505 Games announces them. Steam lists controller input in the requirements, but controller support and a Valve Steam Deck compatibility rating are different questions.
