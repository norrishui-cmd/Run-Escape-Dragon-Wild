Nivalis Nights System Requirements

Official minimum and recommended PC requirements for RAM, GPU memory, storage, DirectX, OS and input.

Quick answer: Minimum requirements include 16 GB RAM, a dedicated GPU with at least 6 GB VRAM and 30 GB storage. Recommended graphics use 8 GB VRAM, such as an RTX 2070 or newer, plus an SSD.

Minimum PC specification

Steam lists 64-bit Windows 10 or 11, an AMD or Intel CPU, 16 GB RAM, an AMD or NVIDIA dedicated graphics card with at least 6 GB dedicated VRAM, DirectX 11, broadband internet and 30 GB available storage. Controller or keyboard and mouse input is listed.

Recommended PC specification

The recommended listing keeps 16 GB RAM and 30 GB storage but calls for an 8 GB dedicated GPU, giving RTX 2070 or newer as an example, DirectX 12 and an SSD. The CPU line is not model-specific, so exact frame-rate predictions would be unreliable before independent benchmarks.
