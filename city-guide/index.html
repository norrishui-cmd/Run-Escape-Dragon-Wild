Nivalis Nights City and District Guide

Confirmed information about the cyberpunk city of Nivalis, its districts, docks, security and persistent population.

Quick answer: Nivalis is a cyberpunk city stretching from the ocean to the clouds, with districts, docks, businesses, homes, persistent NPC schedules and a realistic weather system.

A dense simulated city

ION LANDS says thousands of NPCs continue living their lives. The engine tracks distant district schedules, nearby unseen residents and fully visible characters differently, while more than 10,000 world objects retain their state.

Navigation and danger

Players can explore by their own boat, visit docks and move through a district under CorpSec crackdowns. Curfew and security systems add risk. A complete map needs the shipped location names and routes, so this guide does not invent a district list.
