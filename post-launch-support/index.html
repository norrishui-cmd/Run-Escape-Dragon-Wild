Will Nivalis Nights Get Post-Launch Updates?

What ION LANDS has said about updates, feedback and ongoing support after release.

Quick answer: Yes. ION LANDS says it plans to continue supporting Nivalis Nights after release, particularly by improving the game in response to player feedback and suggestions.

What is promised

The commitment is to ongoing support and improvement. It does not currently provide a dated DLC roadmap, named expansions or a guaranteed number of patches.

How this wiki will track updates

Patch pages will record version, release date, official changes, affected guides and fixed issues. Speculative feature wish lists will remain separate from confirmed updates.

Evergreen SEO value

Versioned troubleshooting and change histories become durable search assets only when old information is clearly tied to the patch where it applied.
