Nivalis Nights Furniture

Furniture database plan for the 620 items confirmed by ION LANDS, plus home decoration and persistent world-state details.

Quick answer: ION LANDS reports 620 furniture items and says players can decorate their own homes or customize homes for friends. Individual pages will publish only when item details are known.

Why furniture has search value

Furniture is not merely a marketing bullet. The developer describes polishing furniture assets for memory efficiency and says the persistent world system remembers more than 10,000 objects such as tables, chairs and dishes. This supports deep collection, placement and home-design guides after release.

Furniture entry requirements

Each page should identify the exact item, acquisition source, price or unlock condition, category, placement limits and visual variants. Closely related cosmetic variants should remain on one page when separating them would create thin content.
