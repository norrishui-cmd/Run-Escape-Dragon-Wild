Nivalis Nights Interior Design Jobs Guide

How paid client decorating works, including instructions, taste and completion requirements.

Quick answer: Players can accept paid interior-design work from people around the city. Unlike decorating their own homes, client jobs require following instructions, matching the client’s taste and satisfying specific requirements.

How jobs are found

The August 2025 community blog says potential clients can be encountered in the streets. They are willing to pay someone who can improve their home, but the developer has not disclosed a job board, skill threshold or fixed contact method.

Creative freedom has constraints

Personal apartments allow broad aesthetic freedom. Client spaces add a challenge because the finished design must respect stated taste and furnishing requirements rather than only the player’s preferred style.

What a walkthrough must include

Each released job guide should record the client, starting location, budget, mandatory items or materials, scoring conditions, payment and failure recovery. Unverified decoration guesses should not be presented as required solutions.
