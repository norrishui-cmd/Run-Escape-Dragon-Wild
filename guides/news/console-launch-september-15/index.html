Dragonwilds Console Launch Set for September 15

Published from official information dated 2026-06-05; editorial status reviewed 2026-07-19.

What changed

Jagex has set September 15, 2026 as the 1.0 launch date on PC, PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S, with crossplay announced.

Confirmed details

  • The full release is planned simultaneously across PC, PS5 and Xbox Series X|S.
  • Scorned Wilderness is included in the 1.0 release plan.
  • Crossplay was explicitly confirmed for the announced platforms.

What this means for players

Players comparing editions should treat September 15 as the announced 1.0 date, not the existing Steam Early Access date. Store availability and platform-specific purchase details should still be checked on the relevant official storefront.

Player checklist

  1. Wishlist on the intended console storefront.
  2. Keep the PC client and account details current before launch.
  3. Check official posts for any later change to timing or crossplay scope.

Official source and status

This article summarizes confirmed information and does not add unannounced mechanics, drop rates or release promises. Future-facing features remain labelled as announced until they are live.
