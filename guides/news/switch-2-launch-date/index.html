Dragonwilds Switch 2 Release Date Confirmed

Published from official information dated 2026-06-09; editorial status reviewed 2026-07-19.

What changed

Nintendo Switch 2 joins the September 15, 2026 launch schedule, and Jagex specifically highlights GameChat support.

Confirmed details

  • The announcement describes solo play or a party with up to three friends.
  • Nintendo GameChat is named as a supported communication feature.
  • The Switch 2 version launches on the same announced date as 1.0.

What this means for players

This announcement closes the earlier uncertainty around Nintendo hardware. It does not automatically confirm every technical option or save-transfer path, so players should separate the confirmed platform/date facts from features that have not been detailed.
