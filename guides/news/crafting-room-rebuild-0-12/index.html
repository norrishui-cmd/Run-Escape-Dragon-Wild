Why Dragonwilds Crafting Rooms Changed in 0.12

Published from official information dated 2026-06-17; editorial status reviewed 2026-07-19.

What changed

The 0.12 station overhaul splits gear production by purpose, so older rooms and recipe locations need to be reorganized.

Confirmed details

  • Blacksmith’s Bench handles tools and melee weapons.
  • Fletching Bench handles ranged weaponry.
  • Mystic Forge handles magic weapons and Vestiges, while the Armour Bench handles armour sets.

What this means for players

Jagex warned that existing Fletching Benches and Smithing Anvils would be destroyed on login with their components left for rebuilding. A missing old bench is therefore not automatically lost materials or world corruption.

Player checklist

  1. Collect the refunded station components.
  2. Rebuild the specialized benches needed by your group.
  3. Update recipe bookmarks that still point to the previous general stations.

Official source and status
