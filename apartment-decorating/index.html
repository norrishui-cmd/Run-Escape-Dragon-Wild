Nivalis Nights Apartments and Decorating

Confirmed home ownership, decoration and furniture systems in Nivalis Nights.

Quick answer: Players can buy and decorate apartments or homes, and can also customize homes for friends. The game includes 620 furniture items according to ION LANDS.

Home customization

The official Steam copy treats home decoration as a major low-pressure activity alongside boating, fishing and chess. It refers to multiple homes and customization for friends, although purchase prices and ownership limits are not yet public.

Persistent furniture

Developer technology notes describe more than 10,000 persistent world elements, including tables, chairs and dishes, and a major furniture asset polish. Individual item pages need verified acquisition, cost, category and placement details before indexing.
