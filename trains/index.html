Nivalis Nights Train Guide

How trains are used for inter-district travel in Nivalis Nights.

Quick answer: Trains are one of the automated systems that move players between the city’s large districts, alongside HOVA taxis.

District connections

The developer confirms that district changes use transportation and include loading screens. Within an entered district, players can move freely and explore most business interiors.

What is not public

Station names, lines, fares, schedules and late-night restrictions have not been detailed. The final guide will need an actual route map and access conditions.

Other travel options

Players cannot drive cars, but they can pilot their own boat for water-connected areas. Trains should therefore be documented as one part of a mixed travel network.
