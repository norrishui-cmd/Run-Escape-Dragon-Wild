# Nivalis Nights Wiki — German and Japanese expansion

Completed July 18, 2026.

## Result

- Added 18 German pages under `/de/` and 18 Japanese pages under `/ja/`.
- Indexable canonical URLs increased from 112 to 148.
- Localized pages cover the homepage, guide hub, FAQ, release date, PC requirements, gameplay, businesses, restaurant management, greenhouses, fishing, transportation, locations, Skyhigh Gardens, decorating, characters, relationships, story and platforms.
- Every translated group has reciprocal `en`, `de`, `ja` and `x-default` hreflang annotations.
- Language navigation connects the equivalent English, German and Japanese page.
- Article structured data includes the correct `inLanguage` value; canonicals and Open Graph locales are language-specific.

## Quality controls

- This is a curated multilingual MVP, not a mechanical translation of all 112 English URLs.
- German pages pass the existing visible-word depth gate; Japanese pages use a character-based depth gate suited to text without spaces.
- The audit rejects duplicate sitemap entries, duplicate metadata, broken internal links, missing direct answers, weak depth, placeholder language and canonical/sitemap conflicts.
- Japanese support is described accurately as interface and subtitles; no Japanese full voice claim is made.

Deploy the package, resubmit `/sitemap.xml`, and inspect the German and Japanese home, guides, release date, system requirements and Skyhigh Gardens pages in Google Search Console. Expand the next localized batch only after impressions identify the stronger language and query clusters.
