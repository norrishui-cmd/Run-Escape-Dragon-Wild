Skills & Builds

The clearest skill guides recommend upgrades by job: what the player is trying to do right now.

Build Buckets

  • Gatherer: harvesting speed, stamina, carry efficiency, and tool value.
  • Explorer: mobility, food efficiency, survivability, and safe scouting.
  • Builder: resource economy, station access, storage, and base utility.
  • Combat: defense, healing windows, ranged backup, magic utility, and recovery.
