Does Nivalis Nights Have Loading Screens?

Where loading screens occur when exploring Nivalis districts and interiors.

Quick answer: Yes. ION LANDS says there are loading screens when traveling between the city’s large districts. Within each district, players can freely explore the area and most business interiors.

District boundaries

The city is structured as several large, detailed districts rather than one continuously loaded map. HOVA taxis and trains are the confirmed automated systems for changing districts.

Exploration inside a district

Once inside a district, players can move around freely. Most businesses have explorable interiors, which reduces the need to treat every shop doorway as a separate travel zone.

Performance context

The team has been optimizing loading, memory and NPC materialization. Exact loading times depend on final hardware tests and should not be predicted from the existence of an SSD recommendation alone.
