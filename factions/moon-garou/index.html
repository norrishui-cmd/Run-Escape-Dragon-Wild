Moon Garou

Current answer

The Moon Garou are a live Umbral Sands faction whose quests support the regional story and lead to Magic Carpet access. They follow the River Mother and survived Fuzan’s destruction.

Confirmed attributes

  • Followers of the River Mother.
  • Their civilization was destroyed by Fuzan.
  • They form an alliance with the player with aid associated with Icthlarin.
  • Their quest line is tied to the Magic Carpet mount.
