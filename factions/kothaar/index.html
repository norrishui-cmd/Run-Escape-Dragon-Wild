Live enemy faction

Kot’Haar

Current answer

Kot’Haar are ancient elemental beings encountered in Umbral Sands. Official lore says their fire once had balance but became corrupted and destructive.

Known variants

The 0.12 update names KotHaar-Xil, KotHaar-Kal and KotHaar-Ket among the region’s enemies. Exact combat values and loot can change during Early Access and are not guessed here.

Regional role

The faction adds an elemental threat alongside the Kalphite brood and Fuzan’s influence. Visit the Umbral Sands enemies guide for the wider roster.
