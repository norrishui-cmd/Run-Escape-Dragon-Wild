Dragonwilds 0.12.1 Is the Final Stop Before 1.0

Published from official information dated 2026-07-08; editorial status reviewed 2026-07-19.

What changed

The July roadmap positions 0.12.1 as the bridge between Umbral Sands and the 1.0 Scorned Wilderness raid.

Confirmed details

  • 0.12.1 is planned for the end of July 2026.
  • Agility and combat/movement changes are part of the announced update direction.
  • Kuldra and Scorned Wilderness are tied to the September 15 release.

What this means for players

Players should not merge every roadmap feature into one patch. Some combat items are explicitly described for 0.12.1, while Wands, the special attack bar and the raid belong to the 1.0 plan.

Player checklist

  1. Track the installed subversion after the July update.
  2. Read feature pages for 0.12.1 versus 1.0 labels.
  3. Prepare current gear without inventing unreleased raid requirements.

Official source and status

This article summarizes confirmed information and does not add unannounced mechanics, drop rates or release promises. Future-facing features remain labelled as announced until they are live.
