Full Release Brings PC and Console Crossplay

Published from official information dated 2026-06-05; editorial status reviewed 2026-07-19.

What changed

Jagex plans a simultaneous PC and console full release, with crossplay explicitly confirmed in the main console announcement.

Confirmed details

  • PC, PS5 and Xbox Series X|S are named in the full-release post.
  • Scorned Wilderness is included in the release plan.
  • A separate official post assigns Nintendo Switch 2 the same date.

What this means for players

Crossplay confirmation does not automatically settle cross-progression, server ownership or every platform performance target. Those details need their own official evidence before publication.

Player checklist

  1. Wishlist on the intended platform.
  2. Keep platform-specific claims limited to confirmed features.
  3. Recheck official support pages before launch for account and save details.

Official source and status

This article summarizes confirmed information and does not add unannounced mechanics, drop rates or release promises. Future-facing features remain labelled as announced until they are live.
