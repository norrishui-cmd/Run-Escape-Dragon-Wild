Nivalis Nights Checklists

Spoiler-safe preparation and launch verification checklists for players and completionists.

Quick answer: Before launch, check the date, PC requirements, language and input support. After launch, collection checklists will cover only verified quests, ingredients, meals, furniture and other entities.

Pre-release checklist

Confirm Windows 10/11 64-bit, 16 GB RAM, dedicated GPU memory and 30 GB free space; prefer an SSD for the recommended setup. Check that your chosen language and input method are listed, then wishlist the Steam release.

Completion checklist policy

The developers report 100+ quests, 240+ ingredients, 190 meals and 620 furniture items. Those counts justify future checklists, but completion percentages require exact release-build inventories rather than empty boxes or invented names.
