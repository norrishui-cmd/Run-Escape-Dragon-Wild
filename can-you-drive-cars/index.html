Can You Drive Cars in Nivalis Nights?

Whether cars are drivable and which vehicles players can control in Nivalis Nights.

Quick answer: No. ION LANDS says players will not be able to drive cars. District travel uses HOVA taxis and trains, while the player can directly pilot a boat.

How road travel works

The Q&A describes HOVA taxis and trains as automated systems used to move between districts. That makes them transportation services rather than player-driven vehicles.

The controllable alternative

Boats provide the confirmed hands-on vehicle system. They enable freer exploration of the waters around Nivalis and help players reach additional fishing areas.

Scope of the answer

This answer reflects the developer’s explicit pre-release statement. It does not infer motorcycles, bicycles or other unannounced vehicle types.
