Is Nivalis Nights Coming to Xbox?

Current Xbox Series X|S and Xbox One release status for Nivalis Nights.

Quick answer: No Xbox version is currently announced in the official Nivalis Nights platform listings. Steam and Epic Games Store on Windows are the confirmed storefronts.

No Xbox badge

The official 505 Games game page shows platform icons for Steam and Epic, unlike other publisher pages that explicitly display Xbox when a version exists.

Single-player and cloud badges

Steam Cloud and single-player support are Steam features; they do not indicate Xbox cloud saves, Play Anywhere or Game Pass availability.

Evidence needed

An ION LANDS announcement, Xbox store listing or 505 Games platform update would be required before this page marks an Xbox release as confirmed.
