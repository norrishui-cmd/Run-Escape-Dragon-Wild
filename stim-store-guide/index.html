Nivalis Nights Stim Store Guide

What is officially known about operating a stim store in the Nivalis Nights business system.

Quick answer: A stim store is one of the small businesses named by 505 Games as a possible starting point, alongside food and noodle stands.

Confirmed but not detailed

The publisher’s game page includes the stim store in the business ladder but does not explain its products, ingredients, licenses or relationship to restaurants. Those systems should not be borrowed from Cloudpunk or fan speculation.

Why it deserves its own page

A non-food starting business may have different stock and customer mechanics from restaurants. That distinct search intent justifies a guide once release data is available.
