Nivalis Nights Furniture Vendors Guide

How furniture shops differ and what players can buy for apartments and venues.

Quick answer: Furniture and accessory vendors are distributed around the city, and each offers a distinct assortment. Players use their stock to decorate homes and business venues.

Why multiple vendors matter

The housing preview says different vendors carry unique assortments rather than one universal catalog. That creates a reason to visit districts and compare stock when building a particular room style.

Furniture scope

The May 2026 optimization update reports 620 furniture items in the game. The count supports a large catalog, but does not publish shop inventories, prices or unlock requirements for individual pieces.

Vendor page standard

After release, each vendor entry should list exact location, hours, stock categories, prices, material sets and prerequisites. Rotating inventory should be distinguished from permanent stock through repeated observations.
