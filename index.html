Story and characters

Nivalis Nights has a defined main story with a specific ending, although players can take different paths. Side quests are tied to a cast of more than 130 characters, with choices shaping friendships, rivalries, business partnerships and romances.

Read about the story structure or meet the robot ferryman in the Salt Pete guide.

Business and farming

The management path can begin with a food or noodle stand and grow into bars, restaurants and nightclubs. Players buy ingredients, hire staff, upgrade venues and expand across districts. Rented greenhouses add crops, climate controls and automated irrigation.

Plan business expansion or open the greenhouse guide.

City and travel

Large city districts are freely explored and include most business interiors. HOVA taxis and trains move between districts with loading screens. Cars are not drivable, but players can pilot a boat to reach waterways and new fishing spots.

Compare transportation or see how the open world is structured.

PC and storefronts

The current Steam requirements list 64-bit Windows 10 or 11, 16 GB RAM, 30 GB storage and a dedicated GPU with at least 6 GB VRAM. The recommended tier adds 8 GB VRAM, DirectX 12 and an SSD.

View the full requirements or check the Epic Games Store status.
