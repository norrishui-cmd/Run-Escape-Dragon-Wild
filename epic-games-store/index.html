Nivalis Nights Epic Games Store Release

Current Epic Games Store availability and how its timing compares with the Steam release.

Quick answer: Nivalis Nights has an official Epic Games Store page and is listed as coming to Windows in 2026. The dated September 29, 2026 launch is currently confirmed on Steam; the release trailer says Epic will follow later.

Epic availability

The publisher page shows both Steam and Epic as platforms, and Epic has a wishlist page for the game. The Epic listing still uses some older Nivalis branding and information.

Do not reuse old specifications

Older Epic requirement fields differ from the current Steam requirements. This wiki uses the current Steam PC specification rather than presenting the older Epic numbers as launch requirements.

Date status

Until Epic or 505 Games provides a specific day, the Epic version should be described as 2026 or later than Steam, not automatically assigned the September 29 date.
