# Nivalis Nights Wiki: release SEO roadmap

Current release target: **September 29, 2026 on Steam**. This plan was reviewed on July 17, 2026.

Round-three status (July 18): **112 quality-approved sitemap URLs**. The third round added 20 generated guides across official districts, housing, interior design, vendors, markets, relationships and fishing expansion. See `SEO_ROUND_3_REPORT.md` for the execution record and next ranked backlog.

## Baseline audit

- Uploaded build: 22 canonical indexable URLs plus a 404 page.
- Main quality issue: most leaf pages contain only 100–250 words and several expose editorial placeholders such as “Unknown,” “To be tested,” “launch template,” or instructions about SEO instead of answering players.
- Technical gaps: inconsistent Open Graph/Twitter metadata, structured data on fewer than half of pages, no automated internal-link/metadata audit, and a sitemap whose dates were changed wholesale rather than per meaningful update.
- Strongest evidence base: the official store listing and ION LANDS’ May 2026 development update. The developer reports 100+ quests, 240+ ingredients, 190 meals and 620 furniture items.

## Publishing phases

1. **Foundation (July 17–31):** repair current thin pages, publish evidence-backed hubs, unify metadata/schema, establish automated quality checks, submit the clean sitemap. Target: 40–60 URLs.
2. **Pre-release authority (August):** use official news, trailers and developer posts for confirmed system guides; build launch data schemas and editorial checklists without indexing empty entities. Current: 112 URLs; target: 130–180 only if evidence supports them.
3. **Launch capture (September 1–28):** release countdown, PC readiness, languages, controls, business primers and spoiler-safe FAQs; prepare but do not index entity drafts. Target: 150–250 quality URLs.
4. **Launch sprint (September 29–October 13):** verify names and values in game, publish quests, ingredients, meals, furniture, characters, fish and locations in controlled batches. Target: 900–1,300 URLs.
5. **Depth and completion (October 14 onward):** add tested strategies, upgrades, relationship events, choices, endings, achievements, bugs and patch changes. Reach 2,000 only after all leaves pass the gate.

## Quality gate

An indexable page must have one distinct player intent, a direct answer near the top, unique title/description/H1/canonical, verified facts, useful attributes or steps, breadcrumbs, at least two relevant internal links, appropriate schema and no placeholder language. It must not cannibalize an existing URL. Exact entity pages require official or first-hand release-build evidence.

## Next three actions after deployment

1. Submit `/sitemap.xml` in Google Search Console and inspect the home page, `/databases/`, `/quests/`, `/ingredients/`, `/meals/` and `/furniture/`.
2. Capture official announcements and gameplay footage into structured evidence sheets for the four confirmed large databases.
3. On release day, begin with quest names and ingredient/meal/furniture inventories; publish 50–100 candidates per batch only after automated and representative human review.
