Nivalis Nights Locations and Districts

Location guide hub for the cyberpunk city, docks, districts, businesses, homes and future maps.

Quick answer: Nivalis stretches from the ocean to the clouds. Confirmed spaces include city districts, docks, homes, business venues and waters explored by boat.

District simulation

The developer’s performance system distinguishes NPCs outside the player’s district, unseen NPCs in the current district and visible characters. That confirms districts are a meaningful world structure, but official materials do not yet provide a complete public district list.

Location page standard

A location page must offer a map position or clear route, services, characters, quests, opening conditions and nearby landmarks. Screenshot galleries without navigation value will not be indexed as standalone location guides.
