Scorned Wilderness Leads to the Kuldra Raid

Published from official information dated 2026-07-08; editorial status reviewed 2026-07-19.

What changed

Scorned Wilderness is framed as the culmination of Kuldra’s Saga and the route to the God-Eater rather than another open-ended regional expansion.

Confirmed details

  • The raid crosses floating islands damaged by wild anima.
  • Players fight through Kuldra’s attacks before reaching the boss.
  • Jagex describes taking allies as optional, preserving a solo-or-group framing.

What this means for players

The official preview intentionally withholds major raid details to avoid spoilers. Guides should therefore cover confirmed preparation and rewards without inventing encounter phases or claiming an exact walkthrough before release.

Player checklist

  1. Finish current regional and Dragon General progression.
  2. Prepare multiple combat options for a high-end challenge.
  3. Treat unverified phase lists or drop rates as speculation until 1.0 is live.

Official source and status

This article summarizes confirmed information and does not add unannounced mechanics, drop rates or release promises. Future-facing features remain labelled as announced until they are live.
