Contact Quest Known Issues in Dragonwilds 0.12

Published from official information dated 2026-06-23; editorial status reviewed 2026-07-19.

What changed

Jagex lists three distinct Contact! presentation problems, so players should identify the exact symptom before restarting or abandoning the quest.

Confirmed details

  • The map marker can have the wrong location or size.
  • Some Avisk textures may fail to display correctly.
  • The Chamber barrier can be invisible on Medium graphics settings and below.

What this means for players

A wrong marker changes the search area, while an invisible barrier hides an object at the intended progression point. They require different workarounds and neither report proves that the save is permanently blocked.

Player checklist

  1. Reread the active objective and use landmarks.
  2. Raise graphics above Medium temporarily for the Chamber barrier.
  3. Keep the quest and report version, objective and symptom if progress still fails.
