Moon Garou Quests Unlock Magic Carpet Rewards

Published from official information dated 2026-06-22; editorial status reviewed 2026-07-19.

What changed

The Umbral Sands quest line connects the Moon Garou alliance to new resources, rewards, cosmetics and the Magic Carpet mount.

Confirmed details

  • Icthlarin assists the player’s work with the Moon Garou.
  • The Magic Carpet is earned through Moon Garou quests.
  • The Wild Hunt remains required for mount access even if customization options unlock earlier.

What this means for players

A mount skin or customization prompt does not necessarily mean the underlying mount system is fully available on that character. Follow the quest requirement rather than treating the interface prompt as proof of access.

Player checklist

  1. Progress the Umbral Sands quest chain normally.
  2. Complete The Wild Hunt if the mount system is still locked.
  3. Do not discard quest items to resolve a cosmetic prompt.

Official source and status

This article summarizes confirmed information and does not add unannounced mechanics, drop rates or release promises. Future-facing features remain labelled as announced until they are live.
