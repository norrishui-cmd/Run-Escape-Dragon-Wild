# German and French SEO localization

Date: 2026-07-18

## Delivered

- 16 German canonical pages under `/de/`.
- 16 French canonical pages under `/fr/`.
- Localized titles, descriptions, H1s, direct answers, guidance, FAQs, navigation and breadcrumbs.
- Reciprocal `hreflang` for English, German, French and `x-default` on every translated pair.
- Self-referencing canonicals and localized `Article`, `FAQPage` and `BreadcrumbList` structured data.
- Language switcher and localized internal-link hubs.
- Multilingual sitemap integration.

## Controlled rollout

This release localizes the highest-value evergreen hubs and problem-solving topics instead of duplicating all English URLs with thin or partial translations. Expand the next multilingual batch only after Search Console supplies country, query and indexing evidence.

## Validation

- 125 unique canonical sitemap URLs: 93 English, 16 German, 16 French.
- Two consecutive builds completed with identical URL counts.
- SEO audit: 0 failures and 0 warnings.
- Locale and reciprocal-hreflang checks passed.
