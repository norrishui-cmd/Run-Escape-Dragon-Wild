Nivalis Nights Apartment and House Types

The confirmed studio, loft and standalone house options for player housing.

Quick answer: Official housing information names three scales of home: a minimal studio apartment, a cozy loft and a standalone house on Calypso Island. Different city districts also have their own styles.

Studio apartments

A studio is the smallest housing example named by ION LANDS. The preview does not provide floor area, room count, rent or furniture capacity, so “minimal” should not be converted into unsupported numerical comparisons.

Lofts and standalone houses

Lofts provide a middle example, while the Calypso Island house represents a separate residential building. The Q&A also confirms apartments and standalone houses across multiple districts as property types players can obtain.

How to compare homes at launch

Useful comparisons should measure total cost, usable placement space, storage, nearby transport and services. Visual size alone is not enough to declare a best home, especially when location changes travel convenience.
