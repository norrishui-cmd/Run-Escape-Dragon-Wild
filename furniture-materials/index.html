Nivalis Nights Furniture Materials and Sets

What official housing previews reveal about furniture sets, accessories and material-based styles.

Quick answer: Furniture vendors sell sets distinguished by different materials, allowing apartments and venues to take on different visual atmospheres. Exact material names and complete set lists are not public yet.

Materials shape style

ION LANDS describes each furniture set as having different materials that change the atmosphere of a home. The wording confirms stylistic groups, not crafting recipes or stat bonuses.

Where sets can be used

Players can place furniture and decorations in apartments as well as business venues. Some homes begin with basic furnishing, which can then be customized with items found around the city.

Database rules
