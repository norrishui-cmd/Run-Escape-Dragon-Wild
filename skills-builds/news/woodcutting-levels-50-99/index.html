Woodcutting Progression Expanded from 50 to 99

Published from official information dated 2026-06-22; editorial status reviewed 2026-07-19.

What changed

Woodcutting receives new spells, storage and equipment milestones across levels 50–99.

Confirmed details

  • Trunk Totem is listed at level 52.
  • Lumber Storage unlocks at 59 and wood weight reduction at 67.
  • The Lumber Backpack recipe arrives at 78, while Axtral Projection gains a later area upgrade.

What this means for players

These unlocks change both how wood is gathered and how much can be moved per run. Route guides should be labeled by skill band because a level-80 character has different logistics from a newly level-50 gatherer.

Player checklist

  1. Use Trunk Totem only where the gathered trees are useful.
  2. Place Lumber Storage near the processing flow.
  3. Equip the Lumber Backpack when its XP and rare-drop benefits match the trip.

Official source and status

This article summarizes confirmed information and does not add unannounced mechanics, drop rates or release promises. Future-facing features remain labelled as announced until they are live.
