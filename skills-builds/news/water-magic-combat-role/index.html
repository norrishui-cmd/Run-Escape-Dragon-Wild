Water Magic Adds Slows and Support Options

Published from official information dated 2026-06-16; editorial status reviewed 2026-07-19.

What changed

Water Magic expands the magic tree with a control-oriented identity instead of duplicating Fire damage or Air knockdown.

Confirmed details

  • Fire retains widespread damage and burning pressure.
  • Air focuses on targeted control and knockdowns.
  • Water attacks are designed to apply slows, with additional water-themed spell utility.

What this means for players

Magic builds can now choose control based on encounter needs. A Water loadout may improve space management against dangerous enemies even when its immediate damage pattern differs from a pure Fire setup.

Player checklist

  1. Unlock and test Water spells against familiar enemies.
  2. Compare slow uptime with the encounter’s movement demands.
  3. Keep alternate runes and a second style for resistant or fast targets.

Official source and status
