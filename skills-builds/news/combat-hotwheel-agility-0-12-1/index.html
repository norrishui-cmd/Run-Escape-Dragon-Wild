Combat Hotwheel and Agility Changes Previewed

Published from official information dated 2026-06-26; editorial status reviewed 2026-07-19.

What changed

The 0.12.1 preview targets faster item selection and movement while setting up the new Agility skill.

Confirmed details

  • A radial hotwheel is planned for controller and keyboard/mouse.
  • Players can assign quick cast to a spell or item and cycle ammunition in combat.
  • Jump stamina drops from 20 to 10 before further Agility reductions.

What this means for players

These are announced 0.12.1 changes, not safe assumptions for an older live build. Control guides should state the version and avoid instructing players to use shortcuts their client does not yet contain.

Player checklist

  1. Confirm 0.12.1 is installed before looking for the hotwheel.
  2. Rebind quick cast around the preferred spell or item.
  3. Retest jump and fall-damage habits after Agility unlocks.

Official source and status

This article summarizes confirmed information and does not add unannounced mechanics, drop rates or release promises. Future-facing features remain labelled as announced until they are live.
