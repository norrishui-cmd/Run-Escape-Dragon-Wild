Nivalis Nights Relationships

Friendship, enemies, romance and relationship management in the cyberpunk life sim.

Quick answer: Players can forge bonds, make friends and enemies, discover character stories and perhaps find love. Choices are intended to have meaningful effects.

More than romance

Official descriptions repeatedly use “relationships” rather than presenting the system only as dating. The cast includes unusual inhabitants, and story interactions are balanced alongside quests, rewards and the economy.

Future character evidence

Character pages will state whether a person is a friend, rival or romance option only after confirmation. Schedules, gifts, dialogue choices and event flags must be tested or sourced, with spoilers clearly marked.
