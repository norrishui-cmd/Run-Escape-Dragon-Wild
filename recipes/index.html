Recipes Guide

A recipe database should connect every craftable item to ingredients, stations, unlocks, and why the item matters.

Required Recipe Fields

  • Recipe name, item category, and crafting station.
  • Ingredient list with links to resource farming pages.
  • Unlock condition, tool tier, or quest requirement.
  • Use case: survival, combat, building, exploration, cooking, or progression gate.
