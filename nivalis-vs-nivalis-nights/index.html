Nivalis vs Nivalis Nights

Why the game formerly called Nivalis is now named Nivalis Nights.

Quick answer: Nivalis Nights is the same ION LANDS game previously called Nivalis. The developer announced the new title and logo on June 30, 2026 after resolving a legal matter.

What changed

ION LANDS says marketing had been paused while it handled a legal matter with a third party. Once resolved, the team announced the fresh title, logo and September 29, 2026 release date. The underlying game did not become a sequel or separate project.

How to read older information

Older trailers, store links and discussions may still use Nivalis. They can remain relevant when they refer to Steam app 1488490 or the same ION LANDS project, but dates and features should be rechecked against current official pages.
