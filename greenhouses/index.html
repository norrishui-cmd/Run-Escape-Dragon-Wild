Nivalis Nights Greenhouse Guide

How greenhouse rental, crop growing, equipment, skills and restaurant supply work in Nivalis Nights.

Quick answer: Players can rent and manage multiple high-tech greenhouses around Nivalis. They can equip and customize them, grow crops from seeds and either sell the harvest or use it as restaurant inventory.

Why farming uses greenhouses

Open land is scarce in the industrial city, so farming is placed inside high-tech structures across the map. Players with enough money can rent more than one greenhouse and expand as their farming skills and seed access improve.

Equipment and progression

Specialized machinery comes from different city vendors. Advanced modules unlock over time, while the farming console controls growing conditions and some processes such as irrigation can be automated.

Business connection

Harvested crops can be sold directly or moved into restaurant supply. The developer specifically positions greenhouse production as a way to reduce pressure and improve profits when restaurant management becomes more demanding.
