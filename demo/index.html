Dragonwilds Demo and Access Status

Current answer

The official Steam store page does not list a public Dragonwilds demo as of July 19, 2026. It currently presents the paid Early Access game instead.

What players can access now

Dragonwilds entered Steam Early Access on April 15, 2025. The listing supports single-player and online co-op and requires Epic Online Services. Recheck the official store before relying on this status because a demo or trial can be added later.

Minimum PC requirements shown on Steam

  • Windows 10 64-bit
  • Intel Core i3-8100 or AMD Ryzen 5 1600
  • 8 GB RAM
  • GTX 1060 6 GB, RX 5600 XT 6 GB or Intel Arc A750 8 GB
  • 25 GB available storage

Demo status method

This page reports what the official store currently lists; it does not claim that a demo has never existed or can never appear. Wishlist and official-news links are safer than unofficial download pages.
