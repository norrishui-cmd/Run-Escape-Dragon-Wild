Nivalis Nights Aspect Ratio and 4:3 UI Support

What ION LANDS has said about 4:3 and other display aspect ratios.

Quick answer: ION LANDS says the user interface was designed to support multiple screen aspect ratios, explicitly including 4:3.

Responsive interface

The game contains more than 40 windows, panels and interface elements. The optimization goal is to keep them clear and consistent across display configurations.

What is not confirmed

Support for 4:3 does not by itself confirm ultrawide field of view, multi-monitor behavior, resolution lists or cutscene presentation. Those need settings-menu and gameplay tests.

Launch checks

The final display guide will compare HUD scaling, text readability, letterboxing and field of view at common 16:9, 16:10, 21:9 and 4:3 ratios.
