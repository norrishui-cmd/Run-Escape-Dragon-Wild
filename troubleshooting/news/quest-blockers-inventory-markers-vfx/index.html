Five Quest Blockers Tracked in the 0.12 Issue Log

Published from official information dated 2026-06-23; editorial status reviewed 2026-07-19.

What changed

The 0.12 issue log separates five common quest symptoms, each with a different safe response.

Confirmed details

  • Contact! can have a wrong marker and an invisible Chamber barrier.
  • Beast in the Sand can lose its trail VFX for clients.
  • Lagra can reject items with a full inventory, while a pre-collected Moonstone can confuse Rogue Trader icons.

What this means for players

Restarting an entire quest or deleting a world is disproportionate to these known presentation and inventory-state problems. Diagnose marker, graphics, host/client state and free inventory space first.

Player checklist

  1. Name the exact quest and symptom.
  2. Free one inventory slot before retrying a hand-in.
  3. Use the host, objective text and landmarks when effects or markers are unreliable.
