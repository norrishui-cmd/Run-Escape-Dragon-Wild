Patch 0.12.0.8 Fixes Fuzan and Floor Crashes

Published from official information dated 2026-06-23; editorial status reviewed 2026-07-19.

What changed

Hotfix 0.12.0.8 addresses two serious state problems and an Umbral Sands audio issue.

Confirmed details

  • Kharidian Flax and Redberry floor pieces no longer crash a logout made while standing on them.
  • Fuzan can no longer follow a player out of Umbral Sands after teleporting.
  • Combat music now resolves correctly in Umbral Sands.

What this means for players

Players reproducing one of these symptoms should verify the complete build number before changing worlds or structures. A server on an earlier 0.12 subversion does not include the fix merely because its major version says 0.12.

Player checklist

  1. Update and restart both client and server.
  2. Back up the world before retesting structures.
  3. Confirm the symptom on 0.12.0.8 or later before filing a new report.
