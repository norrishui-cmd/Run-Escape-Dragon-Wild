Nivalis Nights Meals and Recipes

What is known about the 190 meals in Nivalis Nights and how the recipe database will be built after release.

Quick answer: The May 2026 developer update confirms 190 meals. The final database will connect every verified meal to ingredients, preparation requirements and the businesses where it matters.

Meals and the economy

Food is central to the announced business ladder: the player begins with a noodle stand and can progress through ramen bars, restaurants and other venues. A developer example also describes staff serving Nigiri to customers across the city, confirming that meals participate in persistent business simulation.

Recipe page standard

Every meal page must provide a real recipe or unlock method, required ingredients, preparation station, sell price or business role, and related meals. Pages will not be generated from the number 190 alone.
