Is Nivalis Nights Coming to PS5?

Current PlayStation 5 release status for Nivalis Nights.

Quick answer: No PS5 version has been announced by ION LANDS or 505 Games in the current official platform listings. The confirmed platforms are Windows storefronts on Steam and Epic Games Store.

Current official listings

Steam provides the dated September 29, 2026 Windows release. The publisher page shows Steam and Epic logos but does not show PlayStation.

What controller support means

The PC requirements mention controller input, but that is not evidence of a console port. A PS5 page, trailer or publisher platform badge would be needed for confirmation.

Update rule

This status page will change only after an official announcement. Store wish lists, retailer placeholders or fan requests are not platform confirmation.
