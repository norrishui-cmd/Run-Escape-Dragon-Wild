Nivalis Nights FAQ

Quick answers about the release date, platform, game type, businesses, fishing, romance, languages and PC requirements.

Quick answer: Nivalis Nights is a single-player cyberpunk life sim releasing on Steam on September 29, 2026, with business management, relationships, fishing, boating, homes, minigames and story choices.

Who makes Nivalis Nights?

ION LANDS develops the game and 505 Games publishes it. It was previously promoted as Nivalis; the developer says Nivalis Nights is the same game with a new title and logo.

When does Nivalis Nights release?

The Steam version is scheduled for September 29, 2026. Epic Games Store is also announced for Windows in 2026, but its store page does not currently provide the same specific date.

Which platforms are confirmed?

Windows PC is confirmed through Steam and Epic Games Store. No PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo Switch, native macOS or native Linux version currently appears in the official platform listings.

What are the PC requirements?

Steam lists 16 GB RAM, 30 GB storage and at least 6 GB dedicated VRAM for minimum. Recommended graphics call for 8 GB VRAM, such as an RTX 2070 or newer, plus DirectX 12 and an SSD.

How many characters are there?

ION LANDS says there are more than 130 authored characters with goals, backgrounds and personalities. The city separately simulates thousands of ambient NPCs with daily schedules.

How large is the item and quest content?

A May 2026 development update reports more than 100 quests, over 240 ingredients, 190 meals and 620 furniture items. Individual database pages publish only after exact details are verifiable.
