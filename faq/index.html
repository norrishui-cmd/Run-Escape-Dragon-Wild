Release, Platforms & General Play

What is RuneScape: Dragonwilds?

It is a cooperative survival adventure set on Ashenfall, with gathering, building, crafting, skills, exploration and combat. It can be played solo or online with other players and is being developed by Jagex.

Source: Official Steam store page · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Copyable question link: #what-is-dragonwilds

When did Dragonwilds enter Early Access?

The Steam Early Access release date is April 15, 2025. That is different from the announced 1.0 release date, so guides should not use the two dates interchangeably.

Source: Official Steam store page · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Copyable question link: #early-access-release

When is the Dragonwilds full release?

Jagex has announced September 15, 2026 for the 1.0 launch. The plan includes PC, PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S, with a separate official announcement giving Nintendo Switch 2 the same date.

Source: Official console release announcement · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Copyable question link: #full-release-date

Is Dragonwilds coming to PS5 and Xbox?

Yes. PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S are officially announced for September 15, 2026. Older pages saying console versions are unconfirmed are now outdated.

Source: Official console release announcement · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Copyable question link: #ps5-xbox-release

Is Dragonwilds coming to Nintendo Switch 2?

Yes. Jagex announced a Nintendo Switch 2 release for September 15, 2026 and specifically mentioned GameChat support. The announcement describes solo play or a party with up to three friends.

Source: Official Switch 2 announcement · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Copyable question link: #switch-2-release

Will Dragonwilds support crossplay?

Yes. Crossplay is explicitly confirmed in the official PC, PS5 and Xbox full-release announcement. The announcement does not by itself confirm every cross-progression or save-transfer detail.

Source: Official console release announcement · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Copyable question link: #crossplay

Can Dragonwilds be played solo?

Yes. The official store lists single-player, and Jagex describes the adventure as playable alone or with friends. Dedicated-server setup is optional rather than a requirement for solo play.

Source: Official Steam store page · Reviewed 2026-07-19

How many players can join normal co-op?

The official launch messaging describes playing alone or with up to three friends, meaning a four-player party. Dedicated servers use a separate documented capacity of up to six players.

Source: Official Switch 2 announcement · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Which languages does Dragonwilds support?

Steam lists English, French, Italian, German, Spanish from Spain, Latin American Spanish, Brazilian Portuguese, Simplified Chinese, Japanese and Korean for interface and subtitles. The store does not mark full audio support for these languages.

Source: Official Steam store page · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Quests & Progression Blockers

Which quests were added in update 0.11?

The official 0.11 notes list Black Knight’s Fortress, Wanted!, Biohazard and What’s Theirs Is Mine. Players on older clients or servers must update before expecting this quest set.

Source: Official 0.11 update notes · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Copyable question link: #quests-added-0-11

What do Moon Garou quests unlock?

The Umbral Sands content connects Moon Garou quests with new resources, rewards, cosmetics and the Magic Carpet mount. Normal quest prerequisites still apply; seeing a customization does not necessarily unlock the mount system.

Source: Official 0.12.0 update notes · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Copyable question link: #moon-garou-rewards

Why is the Contact! quest marker wrong?

Jagex lists an issue where the Contact! marker can show an incorrect location or size. Use the active objective, landmarks and visible paths instead of treating the entire marked boundary as exact.

Source: Official 0.12 live issues and hotfixes · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Copyable question link: #contact-marker-wrong

Why is the Contact! Chamber barrier invisible?

The Chamber barrier can be invisible on Medium graphics settings and below. Temporarily raise the graphics preset, reload the area and check the intended progression point again.

Source: Official 0.12 live issues and hotfixes · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Copyable question link: #contact-barrier-invisible

Why can’t I see the Beast in the Sand trail?

The Sand VFX trail is a confirmed issue for client players. In co-op, ask whether the host can see it and follow the host and written objective rather than restarting the world.

Source: Official 0.12 live issues and hotfixes · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Copyable question link: #beast-sand-trail

Why won’t Lagra accept the quest items?

Lagra can reject the hand-in when the player inventory is full. Free at least one slot, keep the required quest items, then restart the conversation. Do not discard unique quest objects.

Source: Official 0.12 live issues and hotfixes · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Copyable question link: #lagra-inventory-full

Why are Rogue Trader map icons incorrect?

Collecting a Moonstone before starting Rogue Trader can make its map icons display incorrectly. Keep the item and follow the written objective; do not delete the Moonstone merely to normalize the markers.

Source: Official 0.12 live issues and hotfixes · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Copyable question link: #rogue-trader-moonstone

Why do I have a mount prompt but no usable mount?

Jagex notes that unlocking a mount skin before acquiring the Terrorbird can leave a defunct prompt. Complete The Wild Hunt to gain the underlying mount access even if a customization appeared earlier.

Source: Official 0.12 live issues and hotfixes · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Resources, Recipes & Crafting

Which crafting stations changed in 0.12?

Gear production was separated into the Blacksmith’s Bench for tools and melee weapons, Fletching Bench for ranged weapons, Mystic Forge for magic weapons and Vestiges, and Armour Bench for armour.

Source: Official 0.12.0 update notes · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Copyable question link: #crafting-stations-changed

Did 0.12 destroy old crafting benches?

Jagex warned that existing Fletching Benches and Smithing Anvils would be destroyed on login, with their components left for rebuilding. Collect the refunded materials before assuming they were permanently lost.

Source: Official crafting-station overhaul · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Copyable question link: #old-benches-destroyed

Do Adamant Bolts require Adamant Arrows first?

No, not on a current build. Patch 0.12.0.4 removed that unintended prerequisite. If the recipe still behaves that way, update both the client and dedicated server.

Source: Official 0.12 live issues and hotfixes · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Copyable question link: #adamant-bolts-locked

Which shield belongs in the Fuzan Dragonfire Shield recipe?

The intended component is the Anti-Dragon Shield. Jagex warned players not to consume Dragonblight Shields when the early 0.12 recipe displayed the wrong component.

Source: Official 0.12 live issues and hotfixes · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Copyable question link: #fuzan-shield-component

Which major materials were added in Umbral Sands?

Update 0.12 added Adamant Ore, Yew Wood, Infinity Cloth and Red Dragonhide progression. These support new tools, weapons, ammunition, robes and armour through the revised crafting stations.

Source: Official 0.12.0 update notes · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Copyable question link: #new-materials-0-12

Which Vestige armour sets were added?

The official notes list Lunar Garou and Desert Robes for magic, Kalphite Carapace for ranged and Obsidian for melee. Comparisons should state the intended combat role rather than call one set universally best.

Source: Official 0.12.0 update notes · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Copyable question link: #vestige-armour-sets

When do specialized resource storages unlock?

Ore and Stone Storage unlocks through Mining level 59, while Lumber Storage unlocks through Woodcutting level 59. Both are part of the expanded level 50–99 progression.

Source: Official 0.12.0 update notes · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Copyable question link: #storage-level-59

Where do Ascension Cores come from?

The Nightmare Crucible is the confirmed source. Players find Engrams around Umbral Sands to access the boss-rush-style challenge, which awards Ascension Cores for powerful upgrades ahead of Scorned Wilderness.

Source: Official 0.12.0 update notes · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Skills, Health & Combat

What is the Dragonwilds skill cap?

Update 0.11 raised the cap of existing skills to 99. The cap increase did not mean every higher-level content tier was immediately complete; later updates have continued filling specific progression bands.

Source: Official 0.11 update notes · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Copyable question link: #skill-cap-99

What was added to Mining levels 50–99?

Update 0.12 added Divine Rock, Ore and Stone Storage, larger Rocksplosion effects, gemstone opportunities and ingot-weight reduction across the Mining 50–99 progression.

Source: Official 0.12.0 update notes · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Copyable question link: #mining-50-99

What was added to Woodcutting levels 50–99?

The 0.12 progression includes Trunk Totem, Lumber Storage, wood-weight reduction, the Lumber Backpack and a later Axtral Projection upgrade with three spectral axes.

Source: Official 0.12.0 update notes · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Copyable question link: #woodcutting-50-99

What is the role of Water Magic?

Water Magic expands the magic tree with a control identity centered on slowing enemies and water-themed utility. It complements Fire’s burning pressure and Air’s targeted knockdown role rather than replacing them.

Source: Official Water Magic preview · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Copyable question link: #water-magic-role

Were Wardstones and Wardshield removed?

Yes. Update 0.12 removed Wardstones and Wardshield. Damage now goes directly to health, while discovering Constitution Shrines increases the character’s maximum HP.

Source: Official health and Wardstones rework · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Copyable question link: #wardstones-removed

How does food healing work after 0.12?

Food now restores HP instantly and can provide other perks. This makes food the immediate recovery resource under the revised health model, so older Wardshield-centered survival routes are outdated.

Source: Official health and Wardstones rework · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Copyable question link: #food-healing

Do healing potions still exist?

Healing potions were removed as part of the health and food rework. Stat potions remain. Players should carry suitable food instead of following old guides that depend on healing potions.

Copyable question link: #healing-potions-removed

What combat and movement changes are announced for 0.12.1?

Jagex previewed Agility, a radial hotwheel, quick cast, in-combat rune and arrow cycling, cheaper jumps and more forgiving fall behavior. Verify the installed version before looking for these controls.

Source: Official 0.12.1 combat preview · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Dedicated Servers & Troubleshooting

Which operating systems support dedicated servers?

The official guide requires a 64-bit operating system and supports both Windows and Linux. Home and cloud hosts remain responsible for setup, networking and backups.

Source: Official dedicated-server guide · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Copyable question link: #server-supported-os

How much RAM does a dedicated server need?

Jagex gives a formula of 2 GB base RAM plus 1 GB per player. That means about 4 GB for two players and 8 GB for the documented six-player maximum.

Source: Official dedicated-server guide · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Copyable question link: #server-ram

How many players can a dedicated server support?

The official dedicated-server guide documents support for up to six players as of update 0.11. This is separate from the four-player party wording used in general console launch messaging.

Source: Official dedicated-server guide · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Copyable question link: #server-player-limit

Which port does a Dragonwilds dedicated server use?

The first server uses UDP port 7777 by default. A second instance uses 7778 and later instances continue upward. The chosen UDP port must pass through the host firewall and every router layer.

Source: Official dedicated-server guide · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Copyable question link: #server-port

Which dedicated-server settings are required?

Owner ID, Server Name, Default World Name and Admin Password are required; World Password is optional. Stop the server before editing because changes made while it runs can be lost.

Source: Official dedicated-server guide · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Copyable question link: #server-required-config

Why is my dedicated server not showing?

First compare the complete server and client versions, then search the exact case-sensitive World Name. Confirm all mandatory settings and inspect RSDragonwilds.log before treating it as a port problem.

Source: Official dedicated-server guide · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Copyable question link: #server-not-showing

How do I back up a dedicated-server world?

Stop the server and copy the required .sav files from RSDragonwilds/Saved/Savegames to a separate protected location. Create a dated backup before updates, migrations or world replacement.

Source: Official dedicated-server guide · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Copyable question link: #backup-server-world

How do I fix distorted or blooming trees?

Close the game, back up and delete Engine.ini from %localappdata%\RSDragonwilds\Saved\Config\Windows, then restart so a clean file is generated. Do not delete the entire Saved directory.

Source: Official 0.12 live issues and hotfixes · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Copyable question link: #tree-graphics-fix

Which patch fixes disappearing stations and Splinter server crashes?

Patch 0.12.0.6 fixed crafting and processing stations disappearing on dedicated servers and the Splinter crash. Update, reload and inspect existing locations before rebuilding stations.

Source: Official 0.12 live issues and hotfixes · Reviewed 2026-07-19

Copyable question link: #stations-splinter-server-fix
