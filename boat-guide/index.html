Nivalis Nights Boat Guide

Confirmed boat exploration and fishing features, plus the future map and ownership questions.

Quick answer: Players can explore Nivalis with their own boat and go fishing from the city’s docks and waterways.

What boats are confirmed to do

The official feature list ties the player’s own boat directly to city exploration and fishing. Marketing copy presents the docks as a place to retreat when business or story pressure becomes intense.

Information still needed

The public materials do not yet confirm boat models, prices, upgrades, docking rules or fast travel. At launch this guide will add the acquisition route, controls, dock locations, route map and any storage or fishing differences.
