Nivalis Nights Gameplay Systems

Overview of the confirmed daily life, business, ingredient, meal, fishing, boat, home, relationship, minigame, curfew and story systems.

Quick answer: Nivalis Nights combines a daily life simulation with business management, relationships and a persistent cyberpunk city. Players choose how to spend time rather than following one fixed career path.

Core progression loops

The player can grow from a noodle stand into restaurants and nightclubs, buy or cultivate ingredients, manage staff activity and compete with rivals. Outside business, confirmed activities include fishing, boat exploration, home decoration, character relationships and a chess tournament.

A persistent city

Thousands of NPCs retain schedules across districts and more than 10,000 objects preserve world state. Time of day, realistic weather, curfew security and player choices connect the simulation to story and risk. Exact formulas and unlock orders require release-build evidence.
