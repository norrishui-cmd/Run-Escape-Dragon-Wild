Nivalis Nights HOVA Taxi Guide

What HOVA taxis do and how they fit Nivalis Nights district travel.

Quick answer: HOVA taxis are an automated transportation option for moving between Nivalis districts. Players cannot drive cars themselves.

Confirmed role

ION LANDS groups HOVA taxis with trains as the systems used for inter-district travel. The city’s large districts are freely explored once entered, but changing districts includes a loading screen.

Information still needed

The developer has not published taxi fares, pickup points, route restrictions or unlock conditions. A release guide will map stops and compare travel time with trains and boat routes.

Cloudpunk context

HOVAs are familiar technology in the city of Nivalis, which also appeared in Cloudpunk. Nivalis Nights is nevertheless designed to work as a standalone game.
