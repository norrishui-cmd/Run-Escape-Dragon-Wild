How Much VRAM Does Nivalis Nights Need?

Official minimum and recommended dedicated graphics memory requirements.

Quick answer: Nivalis Nights requires a dedicated AMD or NVIDIA graphics card with at least 6 GB VRAM. The recommended tier calls for 8 GB dedicated VRAM.

Minimum graphics memory

The Steam minimum specifies a dedicated GPU with 6 GB VRAM and DirectX 11. Integrated graphics are not listed as a supported substitute.

Recommended graphics memory

The recommended tier raises graphics memory to 8 GB and gives an RTX 2070 or newer as an example, alongside DirectX 12.

Visual workload

The voxel-built city uses high polygon counts, multiple levels of detail, vegetation, lighting probes and weather effects. Actual resolution and quality presets still need benchmarks.
