How to Meet Interior Design Client Requirements

A pre-release framework for interpreting Nivalis Nights decorating instructions without inventing scoring rules.

Quick answer: Client decorating jobs have explicit instructions and requirements. The exact scoring interface is not yet public, so the safe approach is to separate mandatory constraints from the client’s broader aesthetic taste after release.

Known job conditions

ION LANDS confirms three elements: clients pay for the work, provide instructions and expect furnishing that matches their taste. Completing requirements is tied to leaving the client satisfied.

Do not assume a hidden score

The preview does not confirm star ratings, timed objectives, budget penalties or required percentages. Those familiar housing-game mechanics cannot be attributed to Nivalis Nights without final evidence.

Release checklist

A tested guide will capture the original brief, room boundaries, required categories, accepted alternatives and completion trigger. It will also note whether moving existing furniture affects the result or payment.
