Nivalis Nights Database

Database hubs for the 100+ quests, 240+ ingredients, 190 meals and 620 furniture items disclosed by the Nivalis Nights developers.

Quick answer: The developers have publicly quantified four large content systems: more than 100 quests, over 240 ingredients, 190 meals and 620 furniture items. These hubs are ready to receive verified entries without publishing empty leaf pages.

Confirmed database scale

The May 2026 development update says the user interface must handle over 240 ingredients, 190 meals, 100 quests and 620 furniture items. Together, those four systems can support more than 1,150 useful entity pages once names, values, requirements and locations are verified.

Indexing rule

A category count is not permission to fabricate URLs. Every entity page needs its exact name plus useful attributes: where to obtain it, what it does, prerequisites, costs or rewards, and genuinely related entries. Until those data exist, only the database hubs belong in the sitemap.
