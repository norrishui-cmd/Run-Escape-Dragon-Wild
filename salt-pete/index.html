Salt Pete Character Guide

Who Salt Pete is, where he works and what the developer has revealed about the Nivalis Nights robot ferryman.

Quick answer: Salt Pete is a tall robot ferryman found near the docks. He operates a makeshift ferry across the bay, wears a straw hat and has a cheerful but argumentative personality.

Where to find Salt Pete

ION LANDS introduced him in a May 2025 character spotlight. He works around the docks and offers a cheap crossing on a vessel assembled from riveted sheet metal, although his own joking safety claim is not a reliable success statistic.

Personality and relationships

Salt Pete enjoys hearing passengers’ stories but avoids discussing his own past. He bickers with other harbor robots, including one called Caleb, and may involve the player in that ongoing conflict.

Guide status

His exact schedule, fares, connected quests and relationship outcomes are not yet public. Those fields will be added only after official disclosure or release-build verification.
