Nivalis Nights Curfew Guide

How curfew, security systems and illegal actions work according to the official Nivalis Nights feature list.

Quick answer: Curfew creates a risk layer: players can avoid security systems while operating illegally, including sabotaging competitors.

Confirmed risk and reward

The feature list contrasts legal ingredient growing with illegal sabotage under curfew. It warns that consequences inflicted on competitors can also happen to the player, indicating reciprocal risk rather than consequence-free actions.

Guide boundaries

Exact curfew hours, detection rules, penalties and security routes are not public. A release guide must test them by district and explain safe routing without pretending that pre-release footage reveals final mechanics.
