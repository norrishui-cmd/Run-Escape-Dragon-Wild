Nivalis Nights Download and Storage Size

How much free storage Nivalis Nights requires and what is known about final download size.

Quick answer: Steam lists 30 GB of available storage for both minimum and recommended PC setups. That is a free-space requirement, not a confirmed final download size.

Official storage figure

The current Steam requirements use 30 GB in both tiers. Installation size can differ from compressed download size, and patches may change the final footprint.

SSD recommendation

An SSD is included in the recommended notes but not the minimum notes. The developer has worked on loading, memory allocations and district transitions, all of which are relevant to storage performance.

Launch-day check

The guide will update with installed size and patch overhead from the release build rather than converting the 30 GB requirement into an invented download estimate.
