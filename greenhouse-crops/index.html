Nivalis Nights Crops and Seeds

Confirmed greenhouse crop categories, seed requirements and uses for harvested ingredients.

Quick answer: Confirmed greenhouse outputs include mushrooms, grains and vegetables, while more advanced equipment can even print meat. Crops require the necessary seeds and can be sold or used in restaurants.

Known crop categories

ION LANDS has named mushrooms, grains and vegetables as examples rather than publishing the complete crop list. This page therefore groups confirmed categories without inventing individual plant names or growth times.

Seeds and skills

Players acquire seeds and unlock more options over time with the right skills. Multiple greenhouses can be rented, allowing production to expand beyond one small plot.

What each crop page will need

A future crop entry must show seed source, environmental settings, growth time, yield, sale value and recipe uses. Cosmetic plant pages will not be mixed with productive crops.
