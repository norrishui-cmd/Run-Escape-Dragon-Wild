Adamant Weapons

Current answer

There are eight officially listed Adamant weapon types in the 0.12 content set.

Confirmed weapon list

  • Adamant Crossbow
  • Adamant Dagger
  • Adamant Greataxe
  • Adamant Greatsword
  • Adamant Hammer
  • Adamant Mace
  • Adamant Scimitar
  • Adamant Sword

Crafting context

Adamant also supports armour, a shield and ammunition. For recipe unlock issues, see the Adamant Bolts fix. Values may change during Early Access.
