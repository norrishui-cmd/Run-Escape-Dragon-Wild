Ascension Cores

Current answer

Ascension Cores are awarded through Nightmare Crucible challenges. Official material confirms the source relationship but not a fixed reward quantity for every run.

Confirmed acquisition path

  1. Acquire an Engram.
  2. Enter the Nightmare Crucible.
  3. Complete its challenge conditions.
  4. Collect the activity rewards, including Ascension Cores.

Data caution

Early Access balance can alter difficulty and payout. This page deliberately avoids assigning an unsupported drop rate, guaranteed count or hidden crafting requirement.
