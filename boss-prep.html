Boss Prep

Fight Readiness

Most hard fights are won before they start. Prepare gear, healing, recovery, and an escape plan.

Checklist

  • Repair weapons and armor before entering the fight area.
  • Carry reliable healing food and emergency consumables.
  • Bring a ranged or magic option for safer damage windows.
  • Set a return point and keep inventory space open for rewards.
  • Write down attack patterns and update the page after each patch.
