# SEO Round 3 — Player problems and dedicated servers

Date: 2026-07-18

## Scope

Round 3 adds exact-answer pages for official 0.12 quest blockers, fixed recipe/game bugs and dedicated-server setup. Each issue page states whether the problem is fixed, still known, or has a published workaround.

## New content

- Five quest stuck-point pages: Contact!, Beast in the Sand, Lagra and Rogue Trader.
- Five game/recipe troubleshooting pages: tree graphics, achievements, fishing, Adamant Bolts and Fuzan Shield.
- Nine dedicated-server pages: setup, requirements, UDP ports, configuration, discovery, version matching, world migration, backups and two fixed server bugs.
- One `/troubleshooting/` hub with reverse links from Quests, Updates and Dedicated Servers.

## Quality rule

No speculative walkthrough steps, shrine coordinates, Engram locations, recipe quantities or enemy drops were added. Those subjects remain gated until live evidence is available.
